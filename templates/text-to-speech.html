💡 How it works

Auto Detect: Automatically detects if your text is in English or Hindi, then:
• If English → Speaks directly in English
• If Hindi → Speaks directly in Hindi
Example: "My name is Pabitra" → English speech | "मेरा नाम पबित्रा है" → Hindi speech

Hindi: Always outputs in Hindi:
• If English text → Translates to Hindi and speaks in Hindi
• If Hindi text → Speaks directly in Hindi

English: Always outputs in English:
• If English text → Speaks directly in English
• If Hindi text → Translates to English and speaks in English

Language Detection: Uses Unicode character analysis to detect Hindi vs English text automatically.
